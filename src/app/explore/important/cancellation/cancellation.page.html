What's the KitchenBrigade cancellation policy?

Standard cancellation policy

For a full refund, cancel at least 7 days before the experience is scheduled to start or within 24 hours of purchase.

Rescheduling

You can reschedule the date or time of the experience and up to 72 hours before the experience is scheduled to start.

Cancellation due to an emergency or weather

If the weather creates a safety concern on KitchenBrigade's part, you will be fully refunded.
