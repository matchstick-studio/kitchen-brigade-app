What should I do if someone asks me to pay outside of the KitchenBrigade website?

Don't send them money, and let us know. We'll never ask you to pay for anything outside the website.

Report requests for offsite payments

If one of KitchenBrigade's experience personel asks you to pay him directly or through another company, report it to us.

How to report

Open the KitchenBrigade app and go to the Inbox. Click the + (plus) icon in the menu bar to start a conversation with us.
